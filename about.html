About Us

Goal

Building Dreams Restoring Hope

Mission

To promote change in social structure and community attitudes that impact positivity on young people.

Vision

To transform communities by inspiring young people throughout the world to open their minds, proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ, accept and include people with intellectual disabilities and thereby anyone who is perceive as different.

Our Core Values

  • Diversity

    • We recognize, respect and value the differences among us and believe that acknowledging and celebrating these differences leads to stronger individuals and stronger communities.

  • Strength based principles.

    • Everyone has an opportunity to reach their full potentials with their abilities to achieve their dreams

  • Inclusion

    • We all have a place in the economic, social, political and cultural life of our society and, for our part we enrich our organization through diversity.
